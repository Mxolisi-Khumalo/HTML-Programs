Did you know that honey never spoils?

Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible! This is because honey has natural preservatives such as low moisture content and high acidity, which create an environment hostile to bacteria and microorganisms. So, if you ever find yourself with a jar of honey that's been sitting in your pantry for years, it's still safe to eat!
